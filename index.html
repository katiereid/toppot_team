How Many Donuts Does Your Store Need?

In order to calculate the number of donuts your store needs, per hour, with a daily total, enter in the following information:

  1. Select a shop location or enter in a new one.
  2. Enter the number of hours your shop is open.
  3. Enter the low number (or minimum) on the range of people who walk by hourly.
  4. Enter the high number (or maximum) on the range of people who walk by hourly.
  5. Enter the percent of those who walk by who actually enter.
  6. Finally, enter the average number of donuts a customer orders and click "submit"!
